The Happy Baker

Today we are going to be making donuts! To make donuts, click the rotating donut. In the beginnig you will make one donut per click. As you make donuts, you can use them as currency to buy advantages. There are two advantages that can be purchased. Mulpitlier increases the number of donuts per click and autoclicker will automatically make donuts for you, hence increasing the number of donuts you have.
